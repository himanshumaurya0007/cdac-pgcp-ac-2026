The Indian Army is the land defence force of India and plays a major role in protecting the country. It works under the Ministry of Defence and is known for its discipline, bravery, and dedication. The Army ensures that people of India live in peace and safety.

Soldiers of the Indian Army are posted in very challenging locations such as high mountains, deserts, and border areas. They face extreme weather conditions, including heavy snowfall and intense heat. Despite these difficulties, they perform their duties with great courage and patience.

The Indian Army is divided into different units like infantry, artillery, armoured forces, engineers, and medical teams. Each unit has a special responsibility. Infantry soldiers protect the borders, engineers build roads and bridges, and medical teams care for injured soldiers and civilians during emergencies.

Apart from border protection, the Indian Army helps citizens during natural disasters. During floods and landslides, Army teams rescue people from dangerous areas, provide food and clean water, and set up medical camps. Their quick action saves thousands of lives every year.
